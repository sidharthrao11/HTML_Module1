An HTML entity is a piece of text("string") that begans with an ampersand(&) and ends with an semicolon(;). HTML entities are frequantly used to display reserved charcter (which would otherwise be interputted as HTML code), and invisible charcters (like non-breaking spaces).you can also use HTML charcter entities in place of other charcters that are difficult to type with a stndard keyboard.
